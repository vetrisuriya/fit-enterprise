Whole person wellness for employees
Employers used to focus on health benefits when talking about
health & wellness for employees.
Whole person wellness is more than just absence of illness among employees.
It is about optimizing physical, mental, emotional and social health of
employees to live the best possible quality of life.
Enabling whole person wellness, positively affects business results.
Work better
We have found most employees work better if they are motivated,
emotionally strong and scientifically build on their strengths.
An AI driven anonymous bot based counselling services, will bye pass
the stigma of asking for psychological help. In cases where the employee
needs higher level of support they have access to mental health experts
who can help them deal with their issues. Supporting people on their softer
issue creates a high performance environment.
Employee Health status
While organizations have been evolving rapidly to promote health and wellbeing
at work-place, the environment, lifestyle, and health issues can impede
demonstrable results and health outcomes, more specifically, diabetes,
high blood pressure and other lifestyle diseases.
Providing easy access to preventive health screening for the employees to
address health issues proactively increases chances of preventing diseases
as they might be caught at an early stage. That means lesser absenteeism
and presentation, which directly increases productivity of organization and lessens
the cost of healthcare for employers.
